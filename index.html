Trustworthy AI Framework for Healthcare

Research Project

üéØ The Challenge

Healthcare AI systems face a critical trust problem. Models achieve high accuracy but lack transparency, making clinicians hesitant to adopt them. Additionally, demographic biases can lead to unfair treatment recommendations, potentially harming vulnerable populations. Standard validation metrics like accuracy miss critical issues around fairness, robustness, and interpretability.

The Problem
  • Black-box models reduce clinical trust
  • Hidden biases risk patient safety
  • Missing/noisy clinical data affects reliability
  • Standard validation misses demographic disparities

üí° My Approach

I developed a modular, notebook-based Python pipeline to evaluate ML models against four Trustworthy AI pillars: robustness, fairness, interpretability, and privacy/data governance. Each component was designed to provide actionable insights for healthcare AI deployment.

Replacing Manual Excel with Automated KPI System Across Global Operations

Production System

üéØ The Challenge

A global automotive manufacturer with 20+ plants across NA/APAC and EMEA was tracking operations KPIs manually in Excel. Each region calculated metrics differently, leading to inconsistent reporting in executive summaries. Finance teams in one continent reported different numbers than Operations in another. Monthly reviews were delayed as stakeholders debated whose calculations were correct.

Business Impact
  • 80+ KPIs with no standardized definitions
  • Manual Excel updates across multiple time zones
  • Inconsistent metrics across 20+ manufacturing plants
  • Executive summaries delayed by data reconciliation

üí° My Solution

I designed and built a comprehensive KPI governance and reporting system with three core components:

üìã
KPI Governance App

Built Streamlit application deployed on AWS as the central intake for KPI definitions, ownership, targets, and manual submissions. Established clear governance: who owns each KPI, how it's calculated, and where it comes from.

‚öôÔ∏è
Data Integration & Automation

Created Snowflake views and scheduled ingestions to unify ODS/ERP data with third-party systems (Enablon for EHS, Dayforce for HR). This enabled automated KPI calculations and created a single source for reporting across all regions.

# Snowflake data pipeline integration CREATE VIEW kpi_warehouse.operations_metrics AS SELECT plant_id, metric_date, metric_value, data_source FROM prod_ods.manufacturing UNION ALL SELECT * FROM ext_enablon.safety_metrics UNION ALL SELECT * FROM ext_dayforce.hr_metrics;
üöÄ
Performance Optimization

Optimized the end-to-end refresh pipeline (Streamlit ‚Üí Snowflake ‚Üí Power BI), achieving dramatic improvements: ~98% reduction in refresh runtime, ~90% lower compute costs, and ~80% smaller model/measure footprint.

üìä Business Impact

98% Faster refresh time
90% Compute cost reduction
100+ Active users
80+ Standardized KPIs

The system transformed operations reporting across the organization. All regions now use standardized KPI definitions with clear ownership and governance. The automated pipeline serves 100+ users across 20+ plants, with monthly executive summaries generated from a single, trusted data source. Manual Excel reconciliation is eliminated.
