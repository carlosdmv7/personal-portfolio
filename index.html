About Me

My name is Carlos De Manuel. I started my journey in data analytics with a passion for turning complex data into clear, actionable insights. Currently, I'm leading analytics initiatives in automotive manufacturing.

Some of my achievements include developing enterprise-wide KPI systems used across three continents, building Power BI dashboards for C-level executives, and creating an open-source framework for Trustworthy AI in healthcare.
